Expt-3: Nature of Speech Signal

  • Load speech for three vowels in the context of heed (for vowel /i/), head (for vowel /e/) and hod (for vowel /a/).

  • Click on the Generate Spectrum button to view its autocorrelation sequence and LP log spectrum.

  • Note the average fundamental frequency (F0) using time domain representation.

  • Note the formant frequencies (F1,Â F2 and F3) from the obtained LP spectrum.

  • Repeat for one more set of vowels.

  • Compare the readings from the two sets (variability within a given speaker).

  • Compare the average fundamental frequency (F0) and formant frequency values of one speaker with values from other speakers (variability among speakers).

  • Write a brief note on the observations.
